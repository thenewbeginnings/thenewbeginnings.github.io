Our Expected Impact

Through our work, we aim to help rural youths and students:

  • Gain access to reliable internet for learning
  • Build basic digital skills for school and future work
  • Access online courses, information, and job opportunities
  • Feel more confident using digital tools

Who Benefits

  • Secondary school students
  • Out-of-school youths
  • Teachers and community learning groups

What Success Looks Like

  • More young people using the internet for learning
  • Improved awareness of online opportunities
  • Stronger digital skills in rural communities
  • Increased hope and motivation among youths
